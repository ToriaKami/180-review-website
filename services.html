My Services

A blend of data-driven SEO and human-centric PR to build trust and authority for your brand.

1. Expert Vetting & Outreach

I identify and vet influential bloggers, journalists, and subject matter experts who align with your brand's values. My focus is on finding authentic voices that will resonate with your target audience, ensuring that product reviews are both credible and impactful.

2. Competitive Landscape Analysis

Using advanced tools like Ahrefs, I perform a deep analysis of the competitive landscape. This data-driven approach allows us to identify high-impact backlink opportunities, benchmark against competitors, and build a PR strategy that is designed for success.

3. Product Seeding Management

I handle the entire product seeding workflow from start to finish. This includes initial outreach, relationship management, shipping logistics, and follow-up. My process is designed to facilitate honest, unpaid reviews that create genuine value and build lasting trust.

4. Digital PR Strategy

I develop comprehensive digital PR strategies that integrate seamlessly with your existing SEO and marketing efforts. The goal is to not only secure high-quality mentions but also to enhance your brand's overall digital footprint and authority.
