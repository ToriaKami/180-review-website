About Me

Hey, I’m Scout.

Long story short: I help good brands earn real trust through honest reviews by real people. No gimmicks. No paid fluff. Just human-to-human storytelling, amplified by smart strategy.

I kind of fell into this line of work by accident.

Back in college, I was freelancing for a tiny indie skincare brand. They couldn’t afford ads, so I started cold-emailing bloggers to see if they’d try the products and write about them.

A few actually did—and their posts moved more product in a week than the brand had sold in months. I’ve been chasing that kind of magic ever since.

These days, I work with e-commerce businesses—especially the scrappy, underdog ones—to get their products into the hands of the right reviewers. I spend way too much time in Ahrefs, stalking competitor backlinks like it’s a full-time sport, and I’m slightly obsessive about vetting bloggers (my “no fluff zone” is a real thing).

If your product’s good and your story’s real, I’ll help make sure the right people talk about it.

Outside of work, I’m trying to get better at making dumplings from scratch (so far, they all look like mini pillows of regret).

My partner, Ella, is a birdwatcher who swears she once saw a rare black-collared hawk in our backyard—I remain skeptical. We live with a 14-pound cat named Raisin, who only ever knocks things off the counter when I’m on Zoom calls. Classic.

This whole journey? Still a work in progress. But I'm loving every single part of it.
